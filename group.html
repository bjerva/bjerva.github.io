Research group

AAU-NLP

We are building a research environment where expertise in linguistics, semantics, machine learning, knowledge representation, privacy, and security can meet around shared problems.

Our direction

Complementary expertise, shared standards.

AAU-NLP brings together research in multilingual NLP, linguistic structure and semantics, knowledge representation, and the security and privacy of language models. We work across foundational questions, methods, and evaluation.

As group leader, I aim to support intellectual independence, methodological rigour, clear ownership, constructive feedback, and sustainable research practice.

Research environment

Different foundations, shared questions.

01

Linguistics & semantics

Meaning representations, typology, pragmatics, and the structure of language.

02

Machine learning

Language models, multilingual transfer, evaluation, adaptation, and interpretability.

03

Security & privacy

Inversion, poisoning, leakage, adversarial behaviour, and trustworthy deployment.

04

Knowledge & reasoning

Factuality, knowledge graphs, model reasoning, and linguistically grounded analysis.
