Research group

AAU-NLP

The Natural Language Processing research group at Aalborg University.

Research environment

Complementary expertise, shared success.

Formally established in 2025, AAU-NLP brings together linguistics and semantics, machine learning, knowledge representation, security, and privacy. My central principle as group leader is to set everyone up for success: creating the trust, clarity, feedback, resources, and room people need to develop their own scientific identity and do ambitious work sustainably.

We use these complementary foundations to sharpen shared research questions rather than collapsing them into a single approach.

Current members, profiles, and projects are maintained on Aalborg University's research portal; vacancies are advertised through AAU's official portal.
