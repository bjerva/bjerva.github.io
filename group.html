Research group

AAU-NLP

We are building a research environment where expertise in linguistics, semantics, machine learning, knowledge representation, privacy, and security can meet around shared problems.

Our direction

Excellent research emerges from a healthy collective system.

AAU-NLP studies the foundations and applications of secure, privacy-preserving, factually reliable, and multilingual language technology. The group's competitive advantage is not only the talent of individual researchers, but a culture that makes expertise visible, feedback routine, expectations explicit, and collaboration easier.

As group leader, I focus on connecting complementary research traditions and creating the conditions for ambitious work: intellectual independence, methodological rigour, generous feedback, clear ownership, and sustainable pace.

Research environment

Different foundations, shared questions.

01

Linguistics & semantics

Meaning representations, typology, pragmatics, and the structure of language.

02

Machine learning

Language models, multilingual transfer, evaluation, adaptation, and interpretability.

03

Security & privacy

Inversion, poisoning, leakage, adversarial behaviour, and trustworthy deployment.

04

Knowledge & reasoning

Factuality, knowledge graphs, model reasoning, and linguistically grounded analysis.
