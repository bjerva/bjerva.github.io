Research group

AAU-NLP

The Natural Language Processing research group at Aalborg University.

Research environment

Complementary expertise, shared standards.

AAU-NLP brings together linguistics and semantics, machine learning, knowledge representation, security, and privacy. As group leader, I emphasise intellectual independence, methodological rigour, clear ownership, constructive feedback, and sustainable research practice.

We use these complementary foundations to sharpen shared research questions rather than collapsing them into a single approach.

Current members, profiles, and projects are maintained on Aalborg University's research portal.
