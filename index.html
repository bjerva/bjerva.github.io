about me

I'm a PhD student in Computational Semantics at the University of Groningen working within the Parallel Meaning Bank project supervised by Johan Bos, and co-supervised by Barbara Plank. I visited the Department of Linguistics at Stockholm University during most of the final year of my PhD. My interests lie mainly in experimenting with deep learning applied to NLP, focussing on multitask and multilingual approaches.
