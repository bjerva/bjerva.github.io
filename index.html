Johannes Bjerva | Computational Semantics

about me

I'm a PhD student in Computational Semantics at the University of Groningen, currently visiting for my final PhD year at Stockholm University. My interests lie mainly in experimenting with various deep learning approaches to NLP, with some forays into the world of digital humanities.
